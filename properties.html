399 Helmericks Avenue

399 Helmericks Ave, Fairbanks, AK 99701

OFD's flagship industrial property sits on 6.25 acres with 66,000 square feet of warehouse and office space across three buildings. Built for heavy use, it offers grade level overhead doors, natural gas heat, and a large gravel pad with room to stage trucks, trailers, and equipment. The location puts tenants minutes from Costco, Walmart, and Home Depot, with easy access for freight and crews.
