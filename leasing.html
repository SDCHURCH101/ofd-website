Leasing FAQ

What types of space does OFD lease?

Industrial and warehouse space at 399 Helmericks Avenue, and downtown office suites at 300 Barnette Street, all in Fairbanks.

Where are the properties?

Both are in Fairbanks, AK 99701. Helmericks is near the major retailers, Barnette is in the downtown core.

How do I find out what is available?

Call (907) 987-2095 or use the contact form. We will share current vacancies, square footage, and terms.

Who manages the buildings?

OFD owns the buildings and Fairbanks Commercial Properties manages them locally, so requests reach a dedicated local team.

Are lease terms flexible?

Yes. Terms and build-outs are shaped around how your business runs, not a one size fits all template.

How is the space heated?

399 Helmericks uses natural gas heat. 300 Barnette is served by efficient district hot water heat.
